Boiled Egg

Description

This is Delia Smith's iconic boiled egg recipe - enjoy!

Ingredients

    An Egg

Steps

  • Before you start: don't boil eggs straight from the fridge! Give them time to adjust to room temperature.
  • Use a kitchen timer or a stopwatch on your phone.
  • Take a small saucepan.
  • Fill it with enough water to cover the egg by 1cm.
  • Bring the water to the boil and when the bubbles are breaking on the surface, gently add the egg.
  • Switch the egg timer on and give the egg 1 minute's simmering time.
  • Remove the pan from the heat, put a lid on it and set the timer again: 6 minutes for a soft-boiled egg, 7 minutes for a firmer yolk.
