Cherry Winks

Description

This is a recipe passed down to me by my Nanny June. I haven't been able to find a recipe similar to hers online, so here is Nanny June's twist on Cherry Winks

Ingredients

    Plain Flour
    Butter/Margarine
    Custard Powder
    Icing Sugar
    Maraschino Cherries

Steps

  • Cream the butter and icing sugar together in a bowl.
  • Add the custard powder and plain flour. Stir.
  • Using floured hands, roll out the mixture into walnut-sized balls.
  • Place the balls on a greaseproof-papered baking tray
  • Press them down with a fork and place a maraschino cherry on top
  • Bake in a moderate (180C fan) oven for 15-20 minutes
  • Enjoy with a cup of tea!
